# Kandle Digital - Design Guidelines

## Brand Positioning
- **Name**: Kandle Digital
- **Industry**: Digital Marketing Agency
- **Market**: Kenyan SMEs & growing brands
- **Tone**: Premium, trustworthy, growth-focused
- **Personality**: Strategic, clear, no fluff

## Design Approach
**Editorial, Confident, Human**: A handcrafted, premium website that feels results-driven. Avoids generic SaaS layouts, stock dashboard visuals, and AI clich√©s.

## Color Palette (Mandatory)
- **Primary/Background**: Deep Charcoal (#0F172A) - hsl(222, 47%, 11%)
- **Accent/CTA**: Warm Amber (#F59E0B) - hsl(40, 96%, 53%)
- **Hover Accent**: Soft Orange (#FB923C) - hsl(27, 96%, 61%)
- **Page Background**: Off White (#F9FAFB) - hsl(210, 20%, 98%)
- **Body Text**: Cool Gray (#475569) - hsl(215, 19%, 35%)

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Plus Jakarta Sans (bold, modern sans-serif)
- **Headings**: Bold/Extrabold, large, confident, editorial
- **Body**: Clean, readable, 400-500 weight
- **Hierarchy**: Strong contrast and generous whitespace
- **Avoid**: Playful or overly techy fonts

## Layout System
- Left-aligned editorial layouts (especially hero)
- Maximum width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6
- Strong hierarchy with whitespace
- No floating cards or badges
- No gradients everywhere
- Use abstract shapes, typography, or real-world visuals

## Hero Section
- Left-aligned editorial layout
- Oversized headline with broken lines
- Headline: "Less Noise. More Results."
- Subheadline: Story-style copy
- Primary CTA (Amber): "Get a Free Growth Plan"
- Secondary (Text/Underline): "See Our Services"

## Page Sections
1. **Hero**: Left-aligned, editorial, oversized typography
2. **About**: Story-style narrative copy, no fluff
3. **Services**: Outcome-based one-line descriptions
4. **Trust**: Kenyan focus, MPESA-friendly, SME emphasis
5. **CTA**: Clear, direct call to action
6. **Footer**: Minimal, WhatsApp & email emphasis

## Component Specifications

**Buttons**: 
- Primary: Amber background, charcoal text, rounded-md, font-bold
- Hover: Soft Orange (#FB923C)
- No excessive shadows or effects

**Cards**: 
- Clean white background
- Subtle border (border-border)
- Hover: Border color changes to amber
- Icon containers: Charcoal background with amber icons

**Motion**: 
- Minimal animations (fade-in, subtle transitions)
- Underline hover effects for links
- No floating or bouncing elements

**Trust Points**:
- Checkmark icons in amber
- Clean card layouts
- Real, outcome-based copy

## Don'ts
- Stock dashboards
- Floating cards or badges
- Gradients everywhere
- Generic buzzwords
- AI-looking layouts
- Playful/techy fonts

## Do's
- Abstract shapes and strong typography
- Real-world business visuals
- Minimal motion
- Strong contrast and whitespace
- Outcome-focused copy
- WhatsApp integration for local market
